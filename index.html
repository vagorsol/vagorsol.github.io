    Graphics

    StyleShade

    Python, GLSL • Spring 2026

    A Maya plugin based on the paper Shading Rig: Dynamic Art-directable Stylised Shading for 3D Characters, creating custome shadow edits to refine toon shading. This tool was made in collaboration with Julie Swei. I implemented the following components:

    • Shading Parameters (movement; gravity & collision; camera control)
    • Interpolation, with transparent textures (water; lava)

    Done as part of CIS 6600: Advanced Topics in Computer Graphics at University of Pennsylvania in Spring, 2026.

    Mini Minecraft

    C++, OpenGL • April 2024

    A simple interactive 3D world mimicking the game Minecraft (Mojang Studios, 2011), made in collaboration with others. I implemented the following components:

    • Player Physics (movement; gravity & collision; camera control)
    • Texturing and Texture Animation, with transparent textures (water; lava)
    • Wave Animation with Blinn-Phong highlights
    • Assisted with assets for procedurally placed assets.

    Done as part of CIS 5600: Interactive Computer Graphics at University of Pennsylvania in Spring, 2024.
