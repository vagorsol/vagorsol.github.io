Mini Minecraft

C++, OpenGL • April 2024

A simple interactive 3D world mimicking the game Minecraft (Mojang Studios, 2011), made in collaboration with others.

I implemented the following components:

  • Player Physics (movement; gravity & collision; camera control)
  • Texturing and Texture Animation, with transparent textures (water; lava)
  • Wave Animation with Blinn-Phong highlights
  • Also assisted with assets for procedurally placed assets.

Done as part of CIS 5600: Interactive Computer Graphics at University of Pennsylvania in Spring, 2024.
